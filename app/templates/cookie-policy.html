What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow certain features to function properly.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website:

  • _ga: Distinguishes unique users
  • _ga_*: Used to persist session state
  • _gid: Distinguishes unique users

Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense may use cookies to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent the same ad from being shown repeatedly

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Google Ads Policy

Opting Out

You can opt out of specific tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Browser add-on
  • Google Ads: Ad Settings

Contact

Questions about our Cookie Policy can be directed to our GitHub repository.
